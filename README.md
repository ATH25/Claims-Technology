## Featured Article

### [The $300 Billion Problem No One Is Solving: A Unified AI Framework for Insurance Claims](https://ath25.github.io/Claims-Technology/)

Insurance fraud costs the US economy over $308 billion annually, yet most carriers are still running fragmented AI tools that cannot talk to each other. This paper makes the case that the performance gap between AI leaders and laggards in insurance claims is not a technology gap — it is an architecture gap. It presents an 18-step unified claims framework organized into five operational phases, covering everything from FNOL intake to continuous model improvement, built around the principle that AI should inform decisions but never make them unilaterally. The paper also addresses the evolving regulatory landscape under NAIC guidance and the transferability of the control architecture across personal lines and group benefits.
